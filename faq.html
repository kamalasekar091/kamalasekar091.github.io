Do I need an appointment to order a cake?

In most cases, no. Unless it is a complicated design/flavour that we cannot get finalized over e-mail / phone, generally there is no need to fix an appointment to order a cake. If you want to sample, yes, you do need to fix an appointment.

Do you offer cake tastings?

Yes. With prior appointments for wedding cakes and orders more than $300. Tastings are available for $25 and include three cake flavors, fillings, and icings of your choice. If the order is placed, the tasting fee can be applied toward your order.

When to order a cake? How far in advance should the order be placed?

Ideally, atleast a month before. We do understand that there might be last minute decision making. So practically, 2 weeks before your event date. Sometimes, we *can* accomodate last minute orders, depending on how busy we are. Please call and check our availability.

What are the payment options?

Payment is due while you pickup/we deliver the cake. In case of cakes more than $200, 25% of the price should be paid in advance as a deposit. For now we accept cash and checks. Please make checks payable to Vidhya Seshadri.

How can I cancel/change my order?

You can always cancel your order one week before and if cancelled any deposit made will be refunded. Most simple changes can be accomodated until a week before. All cancellations/changes must be made in writing.

How can I get a price quote on custom cakes?

Custom cakes are priced based on size, flavor and design/decoration. The price / quote will vary depending on this. After reviewing the cake design/size and decoration we can certainly quote you with the most appropriate price.

Do you ship your cake?

No. Our cakes are made fresh and are very fragile and we do not want to risk it. We do deliver to most areas between Boston, MA and Manchester,NH for a very nominal additional fee. Free delivery to all location within 5 miles from Salem, NH.
